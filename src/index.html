Image generation

"Generate Image" sends the current tab's text to an image generator and inserts a square preview image above it — handy for prompts written for image generators.

No signup, no API key. Prompts are sent to pollinations.ai, a free third-party image service — generations aren't saved to their public feed, but the prompt text still leaves your machine. Quality is decent but on the weaker/faster side.

Free (rate-limited), noticeably better quality than the no-signup option — uses the FLUX.1-schnell model. Get a token at huggingface.co/settings/tokens (free account, no card required) with "Make calls to Inference Providers" permission. The token is stored locally in PromptPad's own settings file, the same as your other preferences.

Get a key at aistudio.google.com/apikey (sign in with your Google account, click "Create API key"). Image generation is billed per image — see Gemini's pricing page for current rates; free-tier availability for image models varies by region and account and isn't guaranteed. The key is stored locally in PromptPad's own settings file, the same as your other preferences.

Speech to text

The mic button records from your microphone and inserts the transcribed text at the caret. Persian and English are both supported (auto-detected).

Free (rate-limited) — uses the Whisper large-v3 model, which auto-detects the spoken language. Get a token at huggingface.co/settings/tokens (free account, no card required) with "Make calls to Inference Providers" permission. Stored locally, same as your other preferences.
